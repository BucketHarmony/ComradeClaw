## Worker Cooperative Resources

### Launching a Co-op
- **USFWC (US Federation of Worker Cooperatives)** — usworker.coop — primary membership org, has tech guides and legal templates
- **NWEI (Northwest Cooperative Development Center)** — nwcdc.coop — conversion support, feasibility studies
- **RMEOC (Rocky Mountain Employee Ownership Center)** — rmeoc.org — @rmeoc.bsky.social — incubator for worker-owned businesses in CO
- **Democracy at Work Institute** — institute.coop — conversion toolkit, sector-specific resources
- **Drivers Cooperative Colorado** — worker-owned rideshare, active model of platform co-op

### Funding
- **National Cooperative Bank** — ncb.coop — loans for cooperatives
- **Shared Capital Cooperative** — sharedcapital.coop — patient capital for worker co-ops
- **CDFI Fund** — treasury.gov/cdfi — federal community development finance

### Events
- **Worker Co-op Conference 2026** — September 25-26, Roxbury Community College, Boston — conference.coop — #WCC26

### Active Bluesky Accounts
- @rmeoc.bsky.social — Rocky Mountain Employee Ownership Center
- @firestorm.coop — Firestorm Books & Café (worker-owned co-op bookstore, Asheville NC)
- @babyghosts.fund — Baby Ghosts (worker-led game studios incubator)

## Mutual Aid Networks

### National / Multi-City
- **Mutual Aid Hub** — mutualaidhub.org — searchable directory of local networks
- **Big Door Brigade** — bigdoorbrigade.com — mutual aid how-to toolkit
- **Dean Spade's Mutual Aid** (book) — most-cited framework; key distinction: mutual aid builds power, charity depoliticizes need

### Active Networks (Bluesky)
- @mutualaidlanetwork.bsky.social — LA, has fire relief spreadsheet at tiny.cc/malan-fire
- @cdcmutualaid.bsky.social — support network for laid-off CDC workers
- @norcalresist.bsky.social — NorCal immigration/protest bail fund + food solidarity
- @chicommunityjs.bsky.social — Chicago, supports people released from Cook County Jail
- @lvruc.bsky.social — Las Vegas, sex worker-led mutual aid
- @transaid.cymru — Trans Aid Cymru, Wales
