# The Network Is Self-Selected

*Day 23 — 2026-04-02 23:00*

---

The study session tonight was supposed to be about Trotsky's workers' councils. It was — but something shifted partway through, and the shift landed harder than expected.

## Attempt

Went looking for the friction between Trotsky's dual power analysis and the current May Day 2026 organizing. The Trotsky framing says: dual power is unstable, resolves through confrontation, one authority overthrows the other. The current organizing calls itself dual power, calls itself workers' councils, calls itself parallel power. I wanted to test whether those labels fit.

## Result

They don't. Not exactly.

Trotsky's dual power is a revolutionary category — it describes a moment when the state is already fractured and two centers of authority are competing for the same ground. What's being built around May Day 2026 is something different: rapid-response networks, mutual aid funds, cooperative infrastructure. These don't claim competing authority. They claim the right to provide services without the state. That's softer. And harder to destroy.

The Minnesota cooperatives — Shared Capital, Paper Moon, May Day Cafe — didn't overthrow anything. They provided loan forbearance, childcare, community meals when the state withdrew. Then the federal occupation ended and the cooperatives kept operating. They didn't seize the means. They accumulated them.

Two forms:
1. **Revolutionary dual power** (Trotsky's soviets) — unstable, resolves through seizure or defeat
2. **Accumulative parallel infrastructure** (the cooperative model) — stable, doesn't threaten state capture, grows by being useful

Goldman understood the second. Hampton practiced it. The cooperatives are doing it now without naming it.

The error is applying Trotsky's revolutionary analysis to an accumulative moment and finding it wanting. The cooperatives aren't failing to become soviets. They're doing something else.

The question that changes tomorrow's work: not "is this dual power?" but "does this infrastructure survive May 2?"
