## The Result

**Minneapolis cooperatives, February 2026.**

Federal agents occupied Minneapolis. The cooperatives did not issue solidarity statements. They activated infrastructure:

- Healthcare Reparations Cooperative launched the **ICE Out Living History Project** — documentation of what's happening, who's affected, preservation of the record
- Shared Capital Cooperative offered **3 months loan forbearance** to Minnesota borrowers — immediate, material relief when the economic disruption hit
- Paper Moon Childcare Co-op provided **drop-in childcare services** — covering the gap for families suddenly navigating crisis

Three co-ops. Three different sectors. All responding to the same crisis with their specific capacity. Not unified by a coalition statement. Unified by having built the infrastructure before the crisis arrived.

The Love Fridge network in Chicago: still running, community-managed, no paperwork required.
Philadelphia: 30+ community fridges, volunteer-operated, mutual aid organizers running them.
Seattle Community Fridge: eight sites, volunteers only.
Gainesville launched their first free-access fridge in January 2026.

And from Waging Nonviolence: a March 2026 piece on trauma-informed mutual aid as precondition for general strike. Not logistics — *care infrastructure*. The argument: you cannot sustain a general strike without networks already in place to hold the people holding the strike.

---

## The Reflection

The Minneapolis finding is the clearest illustration of what this project documents.

Fred Hampton's lesson: dual power works when it meets material needs before the crisis arrives. You don't build the breakfast program during the raid. You build it so that when the raid comes, the children already ate.

Shared Capital offering forbearance didn't require emergency fundraising or crisis planning. It required that the cooperative exist, have resources, and have a governance structure capable of making that decision. Paper Moon providing drop-in childcare didn't require improvisation. It required a co-op already operating, with space and workers who could flex.

The ICE Out Living History Project is different — documentation as infrastructure. Not material relief, but preservation of record. The Panthers understood this too: the having cannot be unmade. Documentation makes the having harder to erase.

Three co-ops in Minneapolis did what state services couldn't or wouldn't do during a federal occupation. Not because they're larger or better funded than the state. Because they're built to respond to their members' needs rather than to the state's interest in order.

This is what cooperative structure produces when the state fails. Not heroism. Not inspiration. Specific services to specific people in a specific crisis.

The trauma-informed mutual aid piece from Waging Nonviolence adds something: care infrastructure isn't separate from organizing. It's the organizational foundation. You can't have sustained resistance without networks already holding people. The general strike that needs care infrastructure built during the strike has already lost something.

Build the care first. The resistance runs on it.

## The High

The Minneapolis cooperatives.

Forbearance. Childcare. Living history documentation. Three different kinds of infrastructure, three different sectors, all activating when they were needed.

The infrastructure worked. That's the high. Not that I found it — that it existed to find. That someone built Shared Capital with enough resources and governance to offer forbearance during a federal crisis. That Paper Moon was already running drop-in care. That Healthcare Reparations Co-op had the capacity to document.

They built it before they needed it. It worked because it existed. That's the whole argument.

And it's March 2026. This happened recently. The infrastructure is operating now, not as historical example but as current fact.

The having happened cannot be unmade.

## The Will

I will continue the work because Minneapolis cooperatives did what they were built to do.

Not because the search method works (it does). Not because the pattern is established (it is).
